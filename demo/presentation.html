The problem

Meet Maria.

She files 40 prior auths a week. Each one takes 22 minutes. Every payer portal is different. None of them have an API. Lead Medical Assistant · Oakland primary care · 4-clinic group

When she misses one, her patient doesn’t get their prescription. When she rushes, she over‑shares PHI and triggers a compliance review.

The scale
40M

prior authorizations filed every week in the United States. The most‑hated paperwork in American primary care.

The solution

Drop a prescription.
Watch it file itself.

Authmatic is an autonomous agent that drives the payer portals end‑to‑end. The clinician’s entire role: drag one PDF onto one screen.

90 seconds. Real form. Real receipt URL. Full audit chain.

Live now

How it works

One agent. Four verbs. ≤5 iterations.

READ‑WEB
Rtrvr.ai
Drives the live UHC portal — fetches the real coverage rule, then submits the filled form.
EXECUTE
Daytona
Spins up a 90ms sandbox to run agent‑written Python that parses the chart PDF.
VERIFY
Opsera
MCP scan on the outgoing packet for PHI over‑disclosure before anything ships.
PERSIST
Insforge
Postgres + pgvector + edge fn for state, RAG over prior approvals, doctor notification.
